Melissa, Co-Founder

Melissa currently serves as Lead Dietitian for a weight management clinic at a major Boston teaching hospital. Her speciality and clinical interest areas are weight management, diabetes, and family nutrition. Melissa understands the demands of every day life and believes food is something to be enjoyed. She is especially skilled at listening to her clients and guiding them toward real solutions for their health concerns. With her colleagues, Melissa has published and participated in primary research, designed community, clinical, and worksite wellness programs, and is a volunteer leader for the Academy of Nutrition and Dietetics Weight Management Practice group. This experience keeps her connected to local and national leaders in the weight management and nutrition community; giving you access to cutting-edge care right in your living room. Melissa is currently completing a Masters of Education that informs best-practice design of programs and educational experiences. Melissa is a Massachusetts native and currently lives in Boston with her husband, Dave, and dog, Lucy (who both benefit a great deal from her cooking adventures at home!) Melissa has been a Registered Dietitian with the Academy of Nutrition and Dietetics and is Licensed to Practice in the State of Massachusetts (#3065) since 2010.
