Melissa Gallagher Landry

Co-Founder

Melissa loves helping people learn and practice healthy eating on their own terms.

As a Registered, Licensed (MA#3065) Dietitian since 2010, Melissa is specialized in weight management, diabetes, and family nutrition. As a person, she understands the demands of every day life. She guides clients toward eating solutions that fit who they are as people, too. The outcome? Enjoyment of food AND the experience of best health!

Melissa is pursuing a Masters of Education that emphasizes online learning design - handy for providing exceptional online experiences for Mission Nutrition clients!

Carissa Burton

Co-Founder

Carissa loves to talk, but more so loves to listen.

Chatting with people and learning about their values and goals is one of her favorite past-times (seriously). This natural ability, plus close to a decade of training in nutrition, dietetics, and health communication has helped those she works with stick to their values while reaching their goals. Working with Carissa can help solve personal and professional puzzles in ways that feel authentic and sustainable.

Carissa's Mission Nutrition focus is communications and consulting, as she recently graduated from Emerson College with a Masters in Health Communication. Carissa will begin seeing individual clients as a Registered Dietitian in January 2015.
