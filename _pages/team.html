Melissa Gallagher Landry

Co-Founder

Melissa loves helping people learn and practice healthy eating on their own terms.

As a Registered, Licensed (MA#3065) Dietitian, Melissa is specializes in weight management, diabetes, and all apsects of family nutrition. As a person, she understands the demands of every day life and guides clients toward eating solutions that fit who they are as people, too. The outcome? Enjoyment of food AND the experience of best health! (Plus, she truly enjoys the ride. Her client's find they have some fun as they make changes for the better!)

Melissa recieved her bachelor's degree from University of New Hampshire in Nutritional Science and a minor in Spanish. She completed her dietetic internship with Sodexo/Southcoast Hospitals Group in 2010. A forever learner, Melissa is currently pursuing a Masters of Education in Instructional Design at the University of Massachusetts, Boston. This degree emphasizes how to effectively build online learning - handy for providing exceptional online experiences for Mission Nutrition clients! Melissa is currently the Lead Dieitian for the Optimal Weight for Life Clinic at Boston Children's Hospital, a Harvard Medical School Affiliate. Melissa remains active in serveral professional organizations as a member of the Massachusetts Dietetic Association and serves as Associate Webinar Director for the Academy of Nutrition and Dietetics.

Carissa Burton

Co-Founder



Carissa’s was hooked on nutrition from her first nutrition course at the University of Oklahoma. Carissa is a people person and noticed that there are few topics that connect us together quite so well as food. After briefly living in Italy where she developed in her ability to connect with others over good food and great conversation, she got to wondering how she could support people in their enjoyment of food and best health. The leap into nutrition consulting was a natural one! Carissa's natural ability to learn about others values and goals, plus close to a decade of training in nutrition, dietetics, and health marketing has helped those she works with stick to their values while reaching their goals. Working with Carissa can help solve personal and professional puzzles in ways that feel authentic and sustainable. Carissa graduated from the University of Oklahoma (Boomer Sooner!) with a Bachelor of Science in Nutritional Sciences and stayed to completed her dietetic internship.She continued her education by earning a Master of Arts in Health Communication from Emerson College. Carissa will be a Licensed, Registered Dietitian in January, 2016. As her day job, Carissa is a Copywriter at Cambridge BioMarketing.
