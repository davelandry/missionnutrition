Melissa

Co-Founder

Melissa is a Registered, Licensed (MA#3065) Dietitian specializing in weight management, diabetes, and family nutrition (including pregnancy, lactation, and child/teen nutrition). This training blends with her natural people-person abilities to create effective, memorable experiences for clients. Melissa believes everyone is capable of reaching their goals with the right support. She helps clients discover ways of eating that serve their physical body, values, and lifestyle needs.

A firm believer that food is meant to be enjoyed, Melissa loves cooking at home with her husband Dave in Roslindale, MA. Their Jack Russell Terrier, Lucy, benefits quite often from what falls to the floor during the cooking process!

  • Favorite Food: Does anyone else find this question unanswerable?!
  • Outside of work: Spinning or yoga, walking Lucy while listening to ‘This American Life’, catching up with friends and family, and of course, enjoying meals with Dave from our "test-kitchen"!
  • Fun Fact: I still and always will love Saturday Night Live.

Carissa

Co-Founder

Carissa has always been drawn to supporting others, especially when it comes to their health. Originally from Oklahoma, Carissa’s love of nutrition began at the University of Oklahoma (Boomer Sooner!) Since then, Carissa has found few other topics that connect her with others as well as sharing in good food and health. After living in Italy and experiencing the impacts of positive food culture first hand, the leap into nutrition communications and consulting was a natural one!

Carissa's ability to learn about others values and goals, plus close to a decade of training in nutrition, dietetics, and health marketing has helped those she works with solve personal and professional puzzles in ways that feel authentic and sustainable.

  • Favorite Food: Gnocchi
  • Outside of work: I like to try new restaurants around Boston, or travel to visit friends and family.
  • Fun Fact: I speak (and have even taught!) Italian
