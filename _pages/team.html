Melissa Gallagher-Landry, Co-Founder

Melissa loves helping people learn and practice healthy eating on their own terms.

As a Registered, Licensed (MA#3065) Dieitian since 2010, her speciality and clinical interest areas are weight management, diabetes, and family nutrition. Melissa understands the demands of every day life and is especially skilled at guiding clients toward solutions for their health concerns that allows them to enjoy food AND eat for their best health. In addition to her pursuit of a Masters of Education, Melissa has co-authored nutrition research, designed effective clinical and worksite wellness programs, and delivered education and training experiences for dieitians. Melissa is a Massachusetts native and currently lives in Boston with her husband, Dave, and dog, Lucy (who both benefit a great deal from her cooking adventures at home!)
