As a Registered, Licensed (MA#3065) Dietitian, Melissa specializes in weight management, diabetes, and all apsects of family nutrition. She understands the demands of every day life and guides clients toward eating solutions that fit who they are. The outcome is great enjoyment of food and the experience of best health! (Plus, she truly enjoys the ride. Her client's find they have some fun as they make changes for the better!) Melissa graduated from the University of New Hampshire with a degree in Nutritional Sciences and a minor in Spanish. She completed her dietetic internship with Sodexo/Southcoast Hospitals Group and has been practicing dietetics since 2010. A forever learner, Melissa is currently pursuing a Master of Education degree in Instructional Design at the University of Massachusetts, Boston. This degree emphasizes effective online learning techniques - handy for providing exceptional experiences for Mission Nutrition clients! Melissa is currently the Lead Dietitan for the Optimal Weight for Life Clinic at Boston Children's Hospital, a Harvard Medical School Affiliate. Melissa remains active in serveral professional organizations as a member of the Massachusetts Dietetic Association and serves as Associate Webinar Director for the Academy of Nutrition and Dietetics.

Melissa loves to cook and practices what she preaches: balancing tasty food, nutrition, and lifestyle (follow her food adventures on our Instagram). Her husband Dave and their Jack-Russell mix, Lucy, are her biggest fans and best taste testers!
