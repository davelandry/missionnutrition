Work with Melissa

Melissa built Mission Nutrition in 2015 after years of working in the over-complicated healthcare system. Her intention? Deliver what works with well designed mobile and online services so her busy, professional clients could learn what works for their health with a little more flexibility and ease.

Clients who work with Melissa are often in the midst of trying to lose weight. After learning all they can on their own, Melissa's expert backgorund and coaching style is a natural next step to keep on keepin' on! By blending weight loss science with personal values, strengths, and lifetyle; Melissa teaches how to confidently move toward health goals and gain a better outlook on life. Melissa models how to:

  • monitor progress through tracking
  • meet weekly, tailored behavior goals to improve health metrics like eating, exercise, sleep, and weight
  • prepare and buy foods (even if you're too busy!)
  • enjoy social eating and favorite foods consistent with health goals
  • adopt a positive, accountable attitude required for long-term success

If you're looking for a fun, flexible, and effective experience with someone whose focused care keeps you motivated, you're in the right spot. That's the mission: Feel better. Enjoy food.
