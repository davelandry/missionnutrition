Work with Melissa

Melissa built Mission Nutrition in 2015 to deliver flexible, online weight loss services to full of life, busy people.

Clients who work with Melissa are often trying to lose weight. After learning all they can on their own, Melissa's expert background and coaching style is a natural next step to keep on keepin' on!

Blending your personal values, strengths, and lifetyle; Melissa models how you can confidently lose weight and gain a better outlook on life through:

  • awareness raising using tracking tools
  • tailored goals that improve health
  • buying and preparing healthy food
  • enjoying socializing consistent with goals
  • adopting a positive, accountable attitude required for long-term success

If you're looking for a fun and effective experience with someone whose focused care keeps you motivated, you're in the right spot. That's the mission: Feel better. Enjoy food.

Credentials
