Work with Melissa

Melissa built Mission Nutrition to deliver flexible, online weight loss services to busy people who want to make the most of their lives.

Clients who work with Melissa are often trying to lose weight. Many have started to learn what works for them. Melissa's expertise and coaching style is a natural next step to keep on keepin' on reaching health goals!

With your personal values, strengths, and lifetyle in mind; Melissa models how to confidently lose weight and gain a better outlook on life through:

  • awareness raising and tracking tools
  • tailored goals that improve health
  • buying and preparing healthy food
  • enjoying socializing consistent with goals
  • adopting a positive, accountable attitude for success

If you're looking for a fun and effective experience with someone whose focused care keeps you motivated, you're in the right spot. That's the mission: Feel better. Enjoy food.
